Internal evidence tool

Launch evidence dashboard

This local dashboard turns a public post URL, platform metrics, and lead/payment checks into copy-ready rows for marketing/campaign-tracker.csv and marketing/evidence-log.csv. It does not post anything and does not count drafts as evidence.

Evidence gates

Qualified visit

Use platform analytics screenshot, public post metrics, or a UTM-tagged sent lead/order email. Test clicks and local curl checks do not count.

Free preview lead

Count only sent email bodies containing lead_type: free_mini_preview_request or free_mini_preview_followup plus UTM fields.

Payment-intent lead

Count sent email bodies containing payment_question_or_intent or order_reservation_intent. Do not count as paid orders.

Paid order

Count only a matching USDT-TRC20 transaction hash, payment receipt, or order email that can be reconciled to payment.
