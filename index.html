FAQ

Before you order.

I was born outside Korea. Is that okay?

Yes. The form asks for birth country and city so the hour pillar can be checked against regional time variables.

Do you always use true solar time?

No. Local civil time is the default. True solar time is checked only when the birth location makes it relevant or when you request it.

What if I do not know my birth time?

A reading is possible, but the hour pillar will be uncertain. Contact us before ordering if your birth time is unknown.

Can I get a refund?

Because each report is personalized, completed reports are not refundable. Input errors can be corrected once before delivery.
