FAQ

Before you order.

I was born outside Korea. Is that okay?

Yes. The form asks for birth country and city so the hour pillar can be checked against regional time variables.

Do you always use true solar time?

No. Local civil time is the default. True solar time is checked only when the birth location makes it relevant or when you request it.

What if I do not know my birth time?

A reading is possible, but the hour pillar will be uncertain. Read the unknown birth time guide before ordering.

When will I receive the PDF?

Most matched orders are processed within 24 hours. If the PayPal notification and birth form do not match, support may ask for the order ID again.

Can I correct my details?

Input errors can be corrected once before delivery. After delivery, a changed birth time, city, timezone, or longitude may require regeneration.

Can I get a refund?

Because each report is personalized, completed reports are not refundable. Input errors can be corrected once before delivery.
