What changes for overseas births

Country and city

The same date and hour can map differently when the birthplace is far from Korea or near a timezone boundary.

Daylight saving time

Some birth records include DST automatically. The report checks whether that matters for the hour pillar.

Longitude

True solar time can shift the practical reading when a city sits far east or west within its timezone.

Recorded civil time

Local civil time remains the default starting point. Adjustments are made only when the context supports them.
